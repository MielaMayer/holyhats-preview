The pitch ↓ See them in the wild
scroll · ocean net in · top-knot out

Ever wanted to wear a bun and a bucket hat? For all of human history you had to pick one. Now you don't.

It's a bucket hat with a hole in the top. Your bun goes through the hole and out the crown — hair's up, face is shaded.

The hole is small on purpose: snug around your bun, your own hair holds the hat down. No chin strap. A gust hits and it stays put — anchored by the magnificent bun God gave you.

"But what if I don't have a bun?" Glad you asked — that's expansion revenue. We'll also sell fake buns: clip-in structural buns so even the bun-less get full hat-retention. We don't just sell the hat — we sell the hair that holds it on.

Made 100% sustainably

You're pulling nets out of the sea & wearing them.

Every HolyHat is made from Return to Vendor (RTV) nylon — spun from recycled ocean fishing nets (~46% of ocean plastic by weight). RTV's breakthrough chemistry rebuilds that nylon over and over with almost no yield lost: infinitely recyclable, PFAS-free, ~70% lower CO₂, at price parity with virgin. The world's first carbon-sequestering bucket hat.
